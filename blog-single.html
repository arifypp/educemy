Top 10 Study Abroad Consultants in India 2024

Educemy Team

Are you planning to study abroad? Are you looking for the best study abroad consultants in India? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss the top 10 study abroad consultants in India. These consultants will help you to choose the best study abroad program and guide you through the entire process of studying abroad.

Studying abroad is a dream for many students. It is an opportunity to explore new cultures, learn new languages, and gain a global perspective. However, the process of studying abroad can be overwhelming. There are many things to consider, such as choosing the right country, university, and course. This is where study abroad consultants come in. They help students to make informed decisions about their study abroad plans and guide them through the entire process.

So, without further ado, let’s take a look at the top 10 study abroad consultants in India.

1. Y-Axis

Y-Axis is one of the leading study abroad consultants in India. They have a team of experienced consultants who provide personalized guidance to students. They offer a wide range of services, including career counseling, university selection, visa assistance, and pre-departure orientation. Y-Axis has a strong network of partner universities and colleges around the world, which makes it easier for students to find the right study abroad program.

2. Edwise International

Edwise International is another popular study abroad consultant in India. They have been in the industry for over 25 years and have helped thousands of students to study abroad. Edwise offers a wide range of services, including career counseling, university selection, application assistance, and visa guidance. They have a team of experienced consultants who provide personalized guidance to students.

3. The Chopras

The Chopras is a well-known study abroad consultant in India. They have a team of experienced consultants who provide personalized guidance to students. The Chopras offer a wide range of services, including career counseling, university selection, application assistance, and visa guidance. They have a strong network of partner universities and colleges around the world, which makes it easier for students to find the right study abroad program.

4. Global Opportunities

Global Opportunities is another popular study abroad consultant in India. They have been in the industry for over 15 years and have helped thousands of students to study abroad.
